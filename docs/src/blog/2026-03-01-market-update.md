## Quick take
- Inventory varies sharply by neighborhood and price band.
- Clean offers still matter, but price realism dominates outcomes.
- Inspections remain a negotiation lever; plan your response strategy in advance.

## For first-time buyers
- Set a ceiling price before emotional escalation.
- Decide up front which issues are deal-breakers vs credits.

## For investors
- Underwrite conservatively: repairs + vacancy + rate variability.
- Favor properties where your downside is bounded.
